Accommodation

There are many hotels and resorts in Daman that cater to all budgets. Some of the popular ones include:

  • The Deltin

      

The price for one night stay ranges from Rs. 7,000 to Rs. 15,000.

  • Sandy Resort

         

The starting price is Rs. 2,000 and the maximum price of the range is Rs. 6,000.

  • Silver Sands Beach Resort

           

The price for one night stay ranges from Rs. 3,000 to Rs. 10,000.

  • Cidade De Daman Beach Resort

        

The starting price is Rs. 4000 and the maximum price of the range is Rs. 10,000.

  • The Gold Beach Resort
      
The price of the hotel ranges from Rs. 7,000 to Rs. 12,000.
